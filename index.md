# Privacy Policy — Raven Health
**Raven Medical Solutions**
Last updated: May 2025

---

## 1. Overview

Raven Health is a personal health tracking application developed by Raven Medical Solutions. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles information entered by its users.

The foundational principle of Raven Health is simple: **your data never leaves your device.** Raven Medical Solutions does not collect, transmit, store, sell, share, or have any access to any information you enter into this application.

---

## 2. Information You Enter

Raven Health allows you to store sensitive personal and medical information including but not limited to:

- Patient names, dates of birth, and demographic information
- Medical history, surgical history, and family history
- Medications, dosages, and medication schedules
- Vital signs and health measurements
- Allergy information
- Emergency contacts and medical providers
- Appointment records
- Photos

All of this information is stored exclusively in a local database on your device. It is never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party under any circumstances.

---

## 3. How Your Data Is Stored

All data entered into Raven Health is stored in a SQLite database located entirely on your device. This means:

- No internet connection is required to use the application
- No account creation is required
- No login to any external service is required
- Your data cannot be accessed remotely by anyone, including Raven Medical Solutions
- Your data is only as secure as your device itself

Raven Medical Solutions has no ability to recover lost data. If you uninstall the application or lose your device, your data cannot be retrieved by the developer.

---

## 4. Device Permissions

Raven Health may request the following device permissions:

**Biometric Authentication / Device Credentials**
The app offers an optional app lock feature using your device's biometric authentication (fingerprint, face recognition) or PIN. This authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system. Raven Health does not have access to your biometric data and does not store any authentication credentials.

**Camera / Photo Library**
The app may request access to your camera or photo library to allow you to add a patient photo. Photos are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted externally.

**Notifications**
The app may request permission to send local notifications for medication reminders and appointment alerts. These notifications are generated entirely on-device and do not involve any external servers.

---

## 5. App Lock and Security — Important Notice

Raven Health offers an optional app lock feature designed to prevent unauthorized access to sensitive patient health information stored within the application.

**By choosing to decline or disable the app lock feature, you acknowledge and accept the following:**

- The health information stored in Raven Health will not be protected by any application-level access control
- Any person who gains physical access to your unlocked device will have unrestricted access to all patient health data stored in the application
- Raven Medical Solutions expressly disclaims all liability for any unauthorized access to, disclosure of, or misuse of health information that occurs as a result of your decision to decline or disable the app lock feature
- The responsibility for protecting access to sensitive health information stored on your device rests solely with you, the user

Raven Medical Solutions strongly recommends enabling the app lock feature, particularly when the application contains information about other individuals in your care.

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

Raven Health does not integrate with any third-party analytics platforms, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or data brokers. No third-party SDKs that transmit data externally are included in this application.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Raven Health is intended for use by adult caregivers and healthcare proxies. The application is not directed at children under the age of 13. Raven Medical Solutions does not knowingly collect any information from children.

---

## 8. Medical Disclaimer

Raven Health is a personal health tracking tool intended to assist caregivers in organizing and monitoring health information. It is not a medical device, does not provide medical advice, and is not a substitute for professional medical care.

Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions. Do not use information tracked in this application as the sole basis for any medical decision.
